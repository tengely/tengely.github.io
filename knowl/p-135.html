Going from right to left, every three base 2 digits can be easily rewritten into base 8, as well. Thus, it is easy to rewrite \(372_8\) into base 2 or \(10101_2\) into base 8:

\begin{align*} 372_8 \amp = 011~111~010_2 = 11111010_2,\\ 10101_2 \amp = 010~101_2 = 25_8\text{.} \end{align*} in-context
