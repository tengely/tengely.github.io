Exercise 2.7.15 It is the same problem as the gold distribution: imagine that everybody of the three siblings gets the brand they like. Then the problem is equivalent to distributing 10 desserts among the three children such that everyone gets at least one. There are \(\binom{10-1}{3-1} = \binom{9}{2} = 45\)-many ways to do this by Theorem 2.7.10.
