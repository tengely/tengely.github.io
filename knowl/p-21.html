The so-called Venn diagrams are often useful in case of sets to understand the situation better. By shading the appropriate region we illustrate the intersection of \(A\) and \(B\text{.}\) \begin{venndiagram2sets} \fillACapB \end{venndiagram2sets} Let \(A=\halmaz{1,2,3,4,5}\) and \(B=\halmaz{3,4,5,6,7}\text{.}\) The intersection of these two sets is the set \(A\cap B=\halmaz{3,4,5}\text{.}\)
