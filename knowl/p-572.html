Let us substitute the formula (2.6.1) into the right-hand side:

\begin{align*} \amp { } \binom{n-1}{k-1} + \binom{n-1}{k}\\ \amp = \frac{(n-1)!}{(k-1)! \cdot (n-1-(k-1))!} + \frac{(n-1)!}{k! \cdot (n-1-k)!}\\ \amp = \frac{(n-1)!}{(k-1)! \cdot (n-k)!} + \frac{(n-1)!}{k! \cdot (n-k-1)!}\\ \amp = \frac{(n-1)!\cdot k + (n-1)! \cdot (n-k)}{k! \cdot (n-k)!} = \frac{(n-1)!\cdot (k + n-k)}{k! \cdot (n-k)!}\\ \amp = \frac{(n-1)!\cdot n}{k! \cdot (n-k)!} = \frac{n!}{k! \cdot (n-k)!} = \binom{n}{k}\text{.} \end{align*} in-context
