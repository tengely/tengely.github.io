Let us consider the product of integers between \(m\) and \(n\text{,}\) where \(m\lt n\text{.}\) We can write it in product notation in different forms:

\begin{align*} \amp \prod_{k=m}^n k,\\ \amp \prod_{m\leq k\leq n}k,\\ \amp \prod_{k\in S}k, \mbox{ where } S=\halmaz{m,m+1,\ldots,n}\text{.} \end{align*} in-context
