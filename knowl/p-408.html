Let \(S(n)\) be a statement depending on \(n\in\mathbb{N}\text{.}\) Let \(m_0 , n_0\geq 1\text{.}\) Suppose that

  1. \(S(m_0),S(m_0+1)\ldots,S(m_0+n_0-1)\) are true,

  2. if \(S(k-n_0+1),\ldots,S(k)\) are true for some \(m_0+n_0-1\leq k\in\mathbb{N}\text{,}\) then \(S(k+1)\) is true.
