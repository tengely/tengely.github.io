\begin{align*} \amp 1 + 7 + 21 + 35 + 35 + 21 + 7 + 1\\ \amp = 1 + (1 + 6) + (6 + 15) + (15 + 20) + (20 + 15) + (15 + 6) + (6 + 1) + 1\\ \amp = 2 \cdot (1 + 6 + 15 + 20 + 15 + 6 + 1 ) = 2 \cdot 2^6 = 2^7 = 128\text{.} \end{align*} in-context
