Now, the question is how to write numbers into different numeral systems. First of all, to write numbers from a numeral system into base 10 we basically calculate the values of the digits using the positional systems, and sum the results:

\begin{align*} 10101_2 \amp = 1 \cdot 2^4 + 0 \cdot 2^3 +1 \cdot 2^2 + 0 \cdot 2^1 + 1 \cdot 2^0 = 16 + 4 + 1 = 21_{10}\\ 1212_3 \amp = 1 \cdot 3^3 + 2 \cdot 3^2 + 1 \cdot 3^1 + 2 \cdot 3^0 = 27 + 18 + 3 + 2 = 50_{10}\\ 372_8 \amp = 3 \cdot 8^2 + 7 \cdot 8^1 + 2 \cdot 8^0 = 192 + 56 + 2 = 250_{10}\\ AFE_{16} \amp = 10 \cdot 16^2 + 15 \cdot 16^1 + 14 \cdot 16^0 = 2560 + 240 + 14 = 2814_{10}\text{.} \end{align*} in-context
